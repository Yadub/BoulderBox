Introduction Course

Coming Soon! For those who have never climbed before or are looking for a refresher. Entry and equipment hire included.

Note: we do not teach belaying on this course.

Technical Training

Coming Soon! Already know the basics, come get some pointers on improving technique for climbing. Join a session appropriate for your level or get a person session focused on yourself.

Belaying Course

Coming Soon! All climbers wanting to belay while rope climbing must be signed off for belaying by one of our members of staff. This course helps you develop the skills to belay safely.

After completion of this course you may belay for 1 week after which you must perform a belay test to be signed for belaying. Note: you cannot do the test before 1 week after the course.
