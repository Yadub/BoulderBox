The Centre

We have fantastic facilities available and we'll be expanding further as we go!

Bouldering

Walls spread over two floors, upto 6m in height. Everything from powerful caves to technical slabs! Bouldering is all about movement and problem solving skills.

Rope Climbing

COMING SOON! Outdoor walls as high as 17m with six anchors and multiple routes each. Rope climbing involves endurance, mental fortitude and technical rope skills.

Moon Board

The Moon Board has over a whopping 8000 problems on it. The Moon Board is a 40-degree bouldering wall to train climbing performance in the simplest of environments.

Cafe

From cookies to healthy salads, we've got you covered. Our coffee beans are from Blue Tokai and our smoothies are made fresh using local produce :)

Exercise Studio

A space to do whatever exercises you may like from yoga to basic weight training. We'll be holding regular classes every week so make sure to sign up early!

Showers

Delhi is hot. Humans sweat. Changing rooms with showers available. Rentable towels also available.

Clean Air

State of the art air-purification and air-conditioning system from Breathe Easy Labs. Escape the Capital's pollution and get fit while doing so!

Green Space

We've had help from the goverment to clean the surrounding areas outside our property. We've taken it upon ourselves to make it green again.

Equipment Hire

Don't have your own gear yet? Not an aspiring gear-junkie? Fear not! We have climbing shoes, chalk, harnesses, and belay devices available for rental hire.
